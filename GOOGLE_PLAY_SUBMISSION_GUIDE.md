# Google Play Store Submission Guide

## Introduction
This guide provides detailed instructions and a checklist for submitting your app to the Google Play Store.

## Pre-Submission Checklist
1. **App adheres to guidelines**: Ensure your app complies with the Google Play Developer Policies.
2. **App function**: Test your app thoroughly to confirm that all features are working as expected.
3. **App size**: Ensure your app size is optimized and meets the Play Store requirements.
4. **Content rating**: Determine appropriate content rating for your application.
5. **Privacy Policy**: Create a privacy policy if your app collects user data.

## Submission Steps
1. **Sign in to Google Play Console**: Use your developer account to access the console.
2. **Create a new application**:
   - Click on "Create Application."
   - Select a default language and enter the title of your app.
3. **Prepare Store Listing**:
   - Fill out the app details, including
     - Title
     - Description
     - Screenshots
     - Feature Graphic
     - Videos
4. **Upload the APK/AAB**:
   - Go to the "Release" section.
   - Click "Create new release" and upload the APK/AAB file.
5. **Set pricing & distribution**:
   - Choose whether your app will be free or paid.
   - Select the countries where you want your app to be available.
6. **Publish the app**:
   - Review the completed sections.
   - Click on the "Publish" button to make your app live.

## Post-Submission Tips
- Monitor app performance and user reviews.
- Regularly update the app to fix bugs and add features.
- Keep an eye on policy changes and adhere to feedback from users.
