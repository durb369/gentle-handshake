# App Name
Gentle Handshake

## Short Description
A seamless way to connect and share.

## Full Description
Gentle Handshake is an innovative app designed to make networking easier and more enjoyable. Whether you're at a conference, meeting, or social event, Gentle Handshake helps you forge meaningful connections effortlessly. With our intuitive interface, you can quickly share your details with others and follow up with ease.

## Features
- Instant connection sharing
- User-friendly interface
- Event reminders and notifications
- Follow up with connections
- Privacy-focused sharing options
- Analytics on networking activity
